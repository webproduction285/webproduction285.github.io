Welcome to the Internet!

The Internet is a theoretical moment in time when artificial intelligence has progressed to the point of a greater-than-human intelligence that has "radically changed human civilization, and perhaps even human nature itself." Since the capabilities of such an intelligence may be difficult for an unaided human mind to comprehend, the Internet is thought of as an occurrence beyond which—from the perspective of the present—the future course of human history is unpredictable or even unfathomable.
